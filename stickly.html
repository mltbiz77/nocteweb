Stickly

A simple, beautiful todo app inspired by sticky notes

Why Stickly?

Stickly helps you stay organized without overwhelming you with unnecessary options or pressure. Just write your todos and check them off when done.

Key features

  • Quick & easy task entry
  • No deadlines or reminders
  • Minimal interface with multiple themes
  • Home- & lock-screen widgets
  • Seamless on-device sync

Pro upgrade

Unlimited todos, extra themes and full widget functionality.
