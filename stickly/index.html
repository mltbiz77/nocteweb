Stickly

A simple, beautiful todo app inspired by sticky notes

Stay Organized Without Pressure

Stickly is a simple and convenient todo app inspired by traditional sticky notes. Write down your tasks and keep them visible until you complete them, no deadlines or complicated features involved.

Features

  • Quick and easy task entry
  • No deadlines or reminders
  • Beautiful, minimal interface and a selection of themes
  • Home screen and lock screen widgets for quick access
  • Seamless syncing within your device

Stickly helps you stay organized without overwhelming you with unnecessary options or pressure. Just write your todos and check them off when done.

Upgrade to Stickly Pro for unlimited todos, extra themes, and full widget functionality.
